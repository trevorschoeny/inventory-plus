# Inventory Plus

Client-side inventory quality-of-life for Fabric. Installs on any server, vanilla or modded.

## Features

- **Auto-Restock** — when your held stack runs out, or a tool or armor piece breaks, automatically refill it from your inventory. Optional "restock before it breaks" swaps in a fresh piece before the current one shatters. Can pull from Inventory Max pockets when Inventory Max is installed.
- **Auto Tool Switch** — hit a block and the right tool swaps into your hand automatically. Optionally switch to a weapon when you attack a mob, with a preferred-weapon setting. Return to what you were holding: off, automatic after a delay, or on a keybind. Hold sneak to suppress. Disabled in creative.
- **Sort** — tidy any container or your inventory with a button or a keybind.
- **Move Matching** — move every matching item between your inventory and a container in one click.
- **Locked Slots** — lock slots so sorting and quick-move leave them untouched.
- **Column Cycler** — turn a vertical column of inventory slots into a cycle you rotate through a hotbar slot, with a mini-hotbar HUD.

Every feature has its own toggle and settings (Mod Menu → Inventory Plus).

Pockets, extra equipment slots, and container locks live in [Inventory Max](https://modrinth.com/mod/inventory-max), the server-side companion mod.

## Requirements

- **MenuKit** 2.0.0+
- **Keybindery** 1.0.2+
- Fabric API. Client-only.
